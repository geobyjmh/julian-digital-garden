**Title:** Business Models Operate Within External Environments

**Core Idea:**  
A business model exists within a broader environment shaped by competitors, technology, regulations, and customer behavior.

**Key Insight:**  
Competition is not part of the business model itself; it is part of the environment surrounding the model.

**Environmental Forces:**

- Competition
    
- Technology shifts
    
- Customer trends
    
- Market changes
