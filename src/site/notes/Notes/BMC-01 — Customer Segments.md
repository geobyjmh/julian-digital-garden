**Concept:** Customer Segments

**Summary:**  
Customer segments are the groups of people or organizations for whom a business creates value. Different segments often have different needs, behaviors, and willingness to pay.

**Key Insight:**  
A business does not serve "everyone." Defining customer segments clarifies who the business is designed to help.

**Questions:**

- Who are the primary customers?
- Are there secondary customer groups?
- Who receives value versus who pays?
