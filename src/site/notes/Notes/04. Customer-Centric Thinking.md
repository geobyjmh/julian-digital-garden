**Title:** Designing Around Customer Needs Rather Than Technology

**Core Idea:**  
Business models should begin with customer problems rather than available technologies.

**Key Insight:**  
Technology becomes valuable only when it addresses meaningful customer jobs.

**Customer Roles:**

- Users
    
- Influencers
    
- Recommenders
    
- Buyers
    
- Decision Makers
    
- Saboteurs
