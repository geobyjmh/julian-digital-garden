**Concept:** Customer Relationships

**Summary:**  
Customer relationships describe the type of interaction a business establishes and maintains with each customer segment.

**Key Insight:**  
Different business models require different relationship types, ranging from self-service to highly personalized support.

**Questions:**

- How much interaction do customers expect?
- What level of support is necessary?
- How can relationships increase retention?
