**Title:** Business Model Thinking: Creating, Delivering, and Capturing Value

**Core Idea:**  
A business succeeds not simply because it has a good product, but because it has a business model that effectively creates value for customers, delivers that value, and captures value in return. Business models should be designed, tested, measured, and continuously improved.

**Key Insight:**  
Product innovation without business model innovation is often insufficient. Competitive advantage increasingly comes from designing superior business models.
