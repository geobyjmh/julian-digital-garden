**Concept:** Revenue Streams

**Summary:**  
Revenue streams explain how a business captures value from customers. They identify where money comes from and the mechanisms used to generate it.

**Key Insight:**  
Creating value is not enough; a sustainable business must also capture part of that value economically.

**Questions:**

- Who pays?
- For what exactly do they pay?
- Which pricing mechanism is used?
