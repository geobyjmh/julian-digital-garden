**Concept:** Key Resources

**Summary:**  
Key resources are the assets required to create and deliver the value proposition, reach customers, maintain relationships, and generate revenue.

**Key Insight:**  
A business model depends on a small set of critical assets that cannot easily be removed without damaging the model.

**Questions:**

- What assets are indispensable?
- Which resources create competitive advantage?
- Which resources are most difficult to replace?
