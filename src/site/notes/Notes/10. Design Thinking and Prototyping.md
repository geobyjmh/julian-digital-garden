**Title:** Business Models Should Be Prototyped Like Products

**Core Idea:**  
Business models should be explored through rapid, inexpensive experiments before committing significant resources.

**Key Insight:**  
The goal of a prototype is learning, not perfection.

**Principles:**

- Explore alternatives
    
- Build quickly
    
- Learn rapidly
    
- Discard weak ideas
