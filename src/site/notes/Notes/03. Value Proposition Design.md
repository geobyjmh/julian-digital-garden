**Title:** Value Propositions Solve Customer Jobs, Pains, and Gains

**Core Idea:**  
A value proposition explains how products and services help customers complete jobs, reduce pains, and create desired gains.

**Key Insight:**  
Customers care about outcomes, not products.

**Example:**  
People do not buy yogurt because it is yogurt; they buy improved digestion.
