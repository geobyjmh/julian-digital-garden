**Title:** Revenue and Cost Structure Determine Viability

**Core Idea:**  
Every business model transforms resources and activities into revenues and costs.

**Components:**

- Revenue Streams
    
- Fixed Costs
    
- Variable Costs
    

**Key Insight:**  
A compelling value proposition is meaningless if revenues do not exceed costs.
