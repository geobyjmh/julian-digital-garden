# Customer Profile

## **1. Customer Jobs**  
Customer jobs are the tasks, problems, or needs that customers are trying to accomplish in their personal or professional lives. These can be functional (completing a task), social (gaining status or approval), or emotional (feeling secure or confident). Understanding customer jobs helps businesses focus on what truly matters to their target audience.

## **2. Pains**  
Pains are the obstacles, frustrations, risks, or negative experiences customers encounter when trying to complete their jobs. These may include high costs, wasted time, complexity, or fear of failure. Identifying customer pains allows organizations to design solutions that reduce or eliminate these challenges.

## **3. Gains**  
Gains represent the benefits, outcomes, or positive results customers desire or expect. These can include improved performance, convenience, cost savings, satisfaction, or unexpected advantages. Understanding gains helps businesses create offerings that exceed customer expectations and deliver meaningful value.

# Value Map

## **4. Products & Services**  
This segment describes the products and services a business offers to help customers complete their jobs. It includes everything from physical products and digital solutions to support services and experiences. These offerings form the foundation of the value proposition.

## **5. Pain Relievers**  
Pain relievers explain how the products or services reduce or eliminate customer pains. They show the specific ways an offering addresses frustrations, risks, costs, or barriers faced by customers. Effective pain relievers directly tackle the issues that matter most to the target market.

## **6. Gain Creators**  
Gain creators describe how the products or services produce benefits and positive outcomes for customers. They highlight the ways an offering creates value, improves experiences, or delivers additional advantages beyond basic expectations. Strong gain creators help differentiate a business from competitors and increase customer satisfaction.
