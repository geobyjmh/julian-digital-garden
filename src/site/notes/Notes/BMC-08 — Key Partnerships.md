**Concept:** Key Partnerships

**Summary:**  
Key partnerships are the external organizations, suppliers, and collaborators that help a business operate effectively.

**Key Insight:**  
No business owns all necessary resources or performs every important activity internally.

**Questions:**

- Which capabilities are sourced externally?
- Which partners reduce risk?
- Which partnerships create leverage?
