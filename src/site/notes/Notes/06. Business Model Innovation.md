**Title:** Business Model Innovation Creates New Competitive Space

**Core Idea:**  
Innovation is not limited to products. Businesses can innovate by redesigning how value is created, delivered, and captured.

**Blue Ocean Actions:**

- Create
    
- Raise
    
- Reduce
    
- Eliminate
    

**Key Insight:**  
The strongest innovations often emerge from changing the business model rather than the product.
