# Core Idea

A hypothesis is an explicit statement describing something that must be true for a business idea to succeed.

# Purpose

Business testing begins by surfacing hidden assumptions and turning them into testable hypotheses.

# Extraction Question

> “What needs to be true for this idea to work?”

### Good Hypotheses Are

- Testable
- Precise
- Discrete

# Example

Weak:

> Customers will like our app.

Strong:

> Busy professionals will use our meal planning app three times per week within the first month.

# Key Insight

Unstated assumptions create hidden risk.
