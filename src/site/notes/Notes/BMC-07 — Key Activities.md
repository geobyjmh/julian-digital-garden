**Concept:** Key Activities

**Summary:**  
Key activities are the most important actions a business must perform to make its business model work.

**Key Insight:**  
Businesses succeed not because they do many things, but because they excel at a few critical activities.

**Questions:**

- What must the organization do exceptionally well?
- Which activities directly create value?
- Which activities can be outsourced?
