**Title:** Understanding Customer Segments Through Jobs-to-Be-Done

**Core Idea:**  
Customer segments are groups of people or organizations for whom value is created. Understanding what customers are trying to accomplish is more important than focusing on demographic characteristics alone.

**Key Insight:**  
Customers "hire" products and services to complete jobs in their lives.

**Customer Jobs:**

- Functional
    
- Social
    
- Emotional
