# Core Idea

**Test Cards** and **Learning Cards** serve different purposes in the Strategizer business testing process.

A **Test Card** is used to design and run an individual experiment.  
A **Learning Card** is used to synthesise evidence and decide what to do next.

---

# Test Cards

## Purpose

A **Test Card** defines:

- what you want to test,
    
- how you will test it,
    
- and what success looks like.
    

## Focus

Experiment execution.

## Structure

1. Hypothesis
    
2. Test
    
3. Metric
    
4. Criteria
    

## Role

A Test Card helps teams run disciplined experiments rather than relying on intuition.

## Example

> We believe busy professionals will pay for meal planning software.

Test:

> Run a landing page with a fake checkout.

Metric:

> Checkout click-through rate.

Criteria:

> At least 10% attempt purchase.

---
# Learning Cards

# Purpose

A **Learning Card** captures:

- observations,
    
- insights,
    
- and decisions derived from evidence.
    

## Focus

Synthesis and decision-making.

## Structure

1. Hypothesis
    
2. Observation
    
3. Learning & Insight
    
4. Decisions & Actions
    

## Role

A Learning Card transforms raw evidence into actionable learning.

---
# Relationship Between Them

Multiple **Test Cards** may contribute evidence toward a single **Learning Card**.

Flow:

> Hypothesis → Test Cards → Evidence → Learning Card → Action

A hypothesis often requires several experiments with increasingly strong evidence before a confident decision can be made.

---
# Key Insight

**Test Cards** generate evidence.  
**Learning Cards** generate understanding and decisions.
