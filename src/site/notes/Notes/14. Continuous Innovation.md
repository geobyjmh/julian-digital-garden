**Title:** Business Models Must Evolve Before They Expire

**Core Idea:**  
Long-term success depends on continuously redesigning and testing business models.

**Key Insight:**  
The best organizations create the next business model before the current one stops working.

**Cycle:**  
Design → Test → Learn → Adapt → Repeat

**Quote:**  
_"Great companies are not those with the best business model today, but those that can repeatedly create better business models tomorrow."_
