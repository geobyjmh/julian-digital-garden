**4DX**, which stands for **The 4 Disciplines of Execution**, is a popular business and personal productivity framework designed to help teams and individuals achieve their most important goals despite the daily distractions of routine work. Popularized by the book of the same name, the methodology is built on four core rules:

1. **Focus on the Wildly Important** by concentrating on just one or two breakthrough goals rather than trying to fix everything at once.
    
2. **Act on Lead Measures** by tracking proactive, predictive actions that drive future success instead of just looking at past results.
    
3. **Keep a Compelling Scoreboard** to visually track progress in real-time.
    
4. **Create a Cadence of Accountability** through regular, focused meetings to review progress and commit to next steps.
      
# Reference

McChesney, C., Covey, S., Huling, J. and Thele, S., 2021. _The 4 disciplines of execution: Achieving your wildly important goals_. 2nd ed. New York: Simon & Schuster.
