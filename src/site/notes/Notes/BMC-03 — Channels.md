**Concept:** Channels

**Summary:**  
Channels are the touchpoints through which a business communicates with customers and delivers its value proposition.

**Key Insight:**  
A great product can fail if customers cannot discover, evaluate, purchase, or receive it effectively.

**Questions:**

- How do customers learn about the offering?
- How do they purchase it?
- How is value delivered after purchase?
