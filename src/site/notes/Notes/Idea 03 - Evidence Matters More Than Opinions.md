# Core Idea

Business testing relies on evidence, not internal belief or customer opinions alone.

# Weak Evidence

- Surveys
- Opinions
- “I would use this”

# Strong Evidence

- Purchases
- Usage behaviour
- Repeat engagement
- Referrals
- Time invested

# Principle

> What people do is more reliable than what people say.

# Types of Evidence

- Qualitative — motivations and behaviours
- Quantitative — measurable outcomes and metrics

# Key Insight

Behavioural evidence is the strongest form of validation.
