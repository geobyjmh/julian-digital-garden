**Title:** Business Models as a Source of Sustainable Advantage

**Core Idea:**  
Companies compete at different levels of business model sophistication.

**Levels:**

- Level 0: Oblivious
    
- Level 1: Beginner
    
- Level 2: Master
    
- Level 3: Invincible
    

**Key Insight:**  
Invincible organizations redesign future business models before current ones decline.
