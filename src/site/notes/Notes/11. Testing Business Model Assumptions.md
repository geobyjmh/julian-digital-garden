**Title:** Business Models Are Collections of Hypotheses

**Core Idea:**  
Every business model contains assumptions that must be tested before scaling.

**Process:**

1. Idea
    
2. Hypothesis
    
3. Experiment
    
4. Insight
    
5. Iteration
    

**Key Insight:**  
There are no facts inside the building; evidence comes from customer interaction.
