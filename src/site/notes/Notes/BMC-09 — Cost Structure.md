**Concept:** Cost Structure

**Summary:**  
Cost structure describes all significant costs required to operate the business model.

**Key Insight:**  
Costs emerge naturally from resources, activities, and partnerships; understanding these drivers reveals how the business consumes value.

**Questions:**

- What are the largest costs?
- Which costs are fixed versus variable?
- How do strategic choices affect cost efficiency?
