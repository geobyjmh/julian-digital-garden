**Title:** Four Common Causes of Business Model Failure

**Core Idea:**  
Most business model failures arise from flawed assumptions rather than poor intentions.

**Four Causes:**

1. Solving unimportant problems
    
2. Weak economics
    
3. External threats
    
4. Poor execution
    

**Key Insight:**  
Failure can often be prevented through early testing and validation.
