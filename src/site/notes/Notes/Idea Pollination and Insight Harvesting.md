If I use the term Zettlekasten to Read, Think, Write about texts, and Digital Garden: to looking for connections and filing notes. What can I call the process of looking for ideas in your text, that is ideas to write about.

# Idea Pollination

This could represent the act of transferring concepts or inspiration from your texts to your writing, analogous to pollination in a garden. – This might involve playing with and sorting indexes.

# Insight Harvesting

Drawing from the metaphor of a Digital Garden, this term suggests gathering ripe ideas from the "garden" of your notes and texts. – This would involve writing papers, blogs, and other written material.
